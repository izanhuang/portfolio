A Pokédex Android app built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, designed to provide a modern and interactive experience when searching for Pokémon details. This project leverages Dagger for dependency injection and Preferences DataStore for lightweight storage.

## **🔧 Features**

- ✅ **Search Pokémon by name or ID**
- ✅ **View detailed Pokémon stats and abilities**
- ✅ **Smooth and responsive UI**

## **📚 Tech Stack**

- **Kotlin (Jetpack Compose)** – Modern UI toolkit
- **Dagger** – Dependency Injection
- **Preferences DataStore** – Persistent key-value storage
