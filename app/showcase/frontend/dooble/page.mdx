Dooble is a simple and fun web app designed to help you get your creative juices flowing. It provides a daily drawing prompt and a straightforward drawing canvas where you can sketch and create. Whether you’re looking to break through a creative block or just have fun doodling, Dooble is the perfect tool for you.

## **🔥 Features**

- 🎨 **Daily Drawing Prompt** – Get a new prompt every day to inspire your creativity.
- 📱 **Mobile and Desktop Support** – Draw and sketch on your phone or computer, wherever you are.
- 💾 **Save & Download Creations** – Keep your artwork by saving it directly to your device.
- 🎯 **Minimal & Focused UI** – A clean, no-distraction canvas to help you focus on drawing.

## **⚙️ Tech Stack**

- **Frontend**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript – Core frontend technologies
- **Canvas Drawing**: HTML5 Canvas API – For smooth and responsive drawing
- **Deployment**: Vercel – For fast and reliable hosting
